Review the following campaign collaboration email and evaluate it carefully.

## Evaluation Criteria
Assess the email across these 5 dimensions:
1. Greeting       — Has a proper, respectful greeting (e.g. "Kinh gui Anh/Chi", "Dear")
2. Timeline       — Clearly states the campaign start and end dates
3. CTA            — Includes a specific call-to-action with a deadline or next step
4. Closing        — Has a professional sign-off (e.g. "Tran trong", "Best regards")
5. Tone           — Overall tone is professional, warm, and appropriate for B2B partnership

## Scoring
- Score 1 point for each criterion that is clearly present and well-executed.
- Score 0 for criteria that are missing or poorly done.
- Total score is 0 to 5.
- "passed" is true if score >= 4, otherwise false.

## IMPORTANT — Output Format
Return ONLY a valid JSON object. No explanation, no markdown, no text outside JSON.

{
  "score": <integer 0-5>,
  "passed": <true or false>,
  "strengths": [
    "<specific thing the email does well>",
    "..."
  ],
  "weaknesses": [
    "<specific thing that is missing or could be better>",
    "..."
  ],
  "improvement_suggestions": [
    "<concrete, actionable suggestion>",
    "..."
  ]
}

If the email is perfect, "weaknesses" and "improvement_suggestions" may be empty lists.
